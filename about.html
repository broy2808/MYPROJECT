Where I've been

The biggest adventure you can take is to live the life of your dreams.--Oprah Winfrey

The phrase keeps popping up in my mind. I am Bony, my journey from a small town in India to the United States is full of ups and downs. I strongly believe you should not settle down in life till you are satisfied with what you are doing. Since childhood, I am an avid reader who loves mathematics and drawing. I used to love solving mathematical puzzles. I thought my interest is in electronics, so joined a University in India to complete my bachelors in Electronics engineering. But soon towards the end of my bachelors, I realized my interest is in Computer Science. So, to pursue my dreams I joined a multinational IT company in India. After spending 2.5 years at that company, I realized this is not the type of Job I am looking for. I would love to learn more about core Computer Science and solve interest problems. So, I started preparing for pursuing a masters degrees.

What I'm doing

I had some experience with C++(Intermediatory level) at my bachelor's degree and worked in ERP, SQL, HTML5, CSS at my previous company. Currently, I am learning Python(Doing a project in Python using Flask framework, basic -4 months) and Javascript. I am revising C++ concepts every day by implementing various data structure and algorithms. Also, my goal is to complete the basics of AWS or cloud computing in general by January-19.
